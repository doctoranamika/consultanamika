ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Dr. Anamika Kumari

Physician, educator, writer, and advocate for evidence-based healthcare.

Dr. Anamika Kumari is an internal medicine physician with a strong interest in preventive healthcare, chronic disease management, public health awareness, and patient education.

Through this platform she shares articles, field observations, case reflections, and commentary on topics ranging from diabetes and hypertension to healthcare delivery, preventive medicine, and community outreach.

Her goal is to make medical knowledge more accessible and to encourage informed conversations about health, wellbeing, and evidence-based care.
