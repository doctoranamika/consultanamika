Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload my medical reports?

Yes. Previous reports, prescriptions, scans, and laboratory results can be shared before the consultation.

Is teleconsultation suitable for emergencies?

No. Medical emergencies require immediate in-person evaluation at the nearest healthcare facility.

Can I request a second opinion?

Yes. Second-opinion consultations are welcome, especially for complex diagnoses or treatment decisions.
