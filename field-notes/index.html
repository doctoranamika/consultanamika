Why Field Notes?

Medicine does not exist only inside clinics and hospitals. Some of the most valuable lessons emerge from conversations in villages, screening camps, outreach programs, and community initiatives.

These field notes document observations, challenges, and opportunities encountered while engaging with communities and public health initiatives.
